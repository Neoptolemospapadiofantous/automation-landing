[Company Name] ("we", "us") provides AI-powered chat assistants that
businesses embed on their websites to answer questions and capture
leads. This page explains how we handle data, how our AI works, and the
protections that apply. It complements our Terms of Service, Privacy
Policy and Cookie Policy.

Last updated: [date] · Contact: [privacy@…] · Entity: [legal name,
registration number, registered address].

**1. Our role.** When a business customer (the "Client") uses our
software with their website visitors, the Client is the data controller
and we act as data processor under a Data Processing Agreement,
processing personal data only on the Client's documented instructions.

**2. You are interacting with AI.** Our assistants are
artificial-intelligence systems. Every chat window states this at the
start of the conversation, and you can ask to speak with a human at any
time — the Client's team is notified immediately.

**3. What we process and why.** Only what's needed to provide the
assistant: your chat messages, contact details you choose to share
(name, email, phone, company), and technical session data. We apply
data minimisation — the assistant captures contact details only when
you provide them.

**4. Automated processing and human oversight.** Our assistants do not
make decisions producing legal or similarly significant effects on a
solely automated basis. You may request human intervention at any time
from within the chat.

**5. Service providers (sub-processors).** Conversations are processed
by leading AI model providers — Anthropic, OpenAI[, and Google] —
together with [Stripe (payments), Pusher (realtime), Amazon Web
Services (email)]. A current sub-processor list is available at [link];
Clients are notified of changes under our DPA.

**6. International data transfers.** Some sub-processors process data
in the United States. Where personal data leaves the EEA we rely on
appropriate safeguards — principally the European Commission's Standard
Contractual Clauses, supported by a Transfer Impact Assessment.
[REVIEW: verify each provider's current mechanism (SCCs vs DPF) before
publishing.]

**7. Security.** Encryption in transit (TLS); model-provider API
credentials held server-side only and never exposed to browsers; rate
limiting on all public endpoints; authenticated, team-scoped dashboard
access; automated daily security scanning (secrets, dependency CVEs);
a tamper-evident billing ledger reconciled daily. [State only measures
in claims-vs-reality.md ✅.]

**8. Your data-protection rights.** Subject to applicable law you may
request access, rectification, erasure, restriction or portability of
your personal data. As processor we forward requests to the relevant
Client (controller) and support them. Contact [privacy@…] or the
business whose website you used.

**9. Data retention.** Chat transcripts are retained per the Client's
instructions and our DPA; idle technical sessions are automatically
deleted after 30 days. [Retention schedule link.]

**10. Cookies.** The chat widget uses a single strictly-necessary
cookie so your conversation survives a page reload (30 days). The
dashboard uses strictly-necessary session cookies. Non-essential
cookies or analytics are used only with consent.

**11. Contact.** [privacy@…] · [DPO if appointed] · You may lodge a
complaint with your supervisory authority.

**12. Changes.** We may update this page; the "last updated" date
reflects the current version.
